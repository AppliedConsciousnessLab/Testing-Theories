# Data Sources
© Applied Consciousness Lab (ACL)

This document lists the primary datasets used to test hypotheses within the Testing-Theories repository.

These datasets support empirical evaluation of the following research frameworks:

- Emotional Physics (EP)
- Global Safety Standard (GSS)
- Societal Thermodynamics Framework (STF)
- Societal Entropy Index (SEI)

All datasets referenced here are publicly available and widely used in academic research.

---

# Global Economic Data

## World Bank

https://data.worldbank.org

Provides global economic indicators including:

• GDP  
• income inequality (Gini Index)  
• unemployment  
• poverty levels  
• trade flows  
• demographic data  

Example dataset used in SEI testing:

World Bank Gini Index (Income Inequality)

---

## International Monetary Fund (IMF)

https://www.imf.org/en/Data

Provides macroeconomic indicators including:

• sovereign debt levels  
• fiscal balances  
• inflation data  
• financial stability metrics  

---

# Institutional Trust and Governance

## World Values Survey (WVS)

https://www.worldvaluessurvey.org

Large-scale international survey measuring:

• trust in government  
• trust in institutions  
• social values  
• political attitudes  
• cultural norms  

This dataset is especially useful for testing **institutional legitimacy variables** within SEI.

---

## Edelman Trust Barometer

https://www.edelman.com/trust

Annual global survey measuring:

• trust in government  
• trust in business  
• trust in media  
• trust in NGOs  

---

## Transparency International

https://www.transparency.org

Provides the **Corruption Perceptions Index**, widely used to measure institutional integrity and governance quality.

---

# Democracy and Political Stability

## Freedom House

https://freedomhouse.org

Measures:

• political rights  
• civil liberties  
• democratic governance indicators  

---

## V-Dem Dataset (Varieties of Democracy)

https://www.v-dem.net

One of the most comprehensive datasets measuring:

• democratic institutions  
• civil liberties  
• political participation  
• governance structures

# Conflict and Geopolitical Data

## Uppsala Conflict Data Program (UCDP)

https://ucdp.uu.se

Provides detailed datasets on:

• armed conflicts  
• battle deaths  
• conflict actors  
• peace agreements  

---

## Correlates of War Project

https://correlatesofwar.org

Provides historical data on:

• interstate wars  
• alliances  
• military capabilities

# Dataset Usage Principles

When datasets are used within experiments, researchers should document:

• dataset source  
• dataset version or year  
• variables used  
• data preprocessing steps  

This documentation ensures transparency and reproducibility.
