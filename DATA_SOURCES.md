# Data Sources
© Applied Consciousness Lab (ACL)

This document catalogs the primary datasets used for empirical research within the **Testing-Theories** repository.

These datasets support the testing and validation of the following research frameworks developed by the Applied Consciousness Lab:

• Emotional Physics (EP)  
• Global Safety Standard (GSS)  
• Societal Thermodynamics Framework (STF)  
• Societal Entropy Index (SEI)

The goal is to ensure that all experiments conducted within this repository rely on **transparent, reputable, and publicly accessible datasets**.

---

# Global Economic Data

### World Bank Open Data
https://data.worldbank.org

Provides global development indicators including:

• GDP growth  
• income inequality (Gini Index)  
• unemployment rates  
• poverty levels  
• trade flows  
• demographic indicators

Example variables used in research:

• Gini Index (income inequality)  
• GDP per capita  
• poverty headcount ratio  

These variables are frequently used when testing **economic stability factors in SEI models**.

---

### International Monetary Fund (IMF)
https://www.imf.org/en/Data

Provides macroeconomic indicators including:

• sovereign debt levels  
• fiscal balances  
• inflation data  
• financial stability metrics

Useful for evaluating:

• macroeconomic resilience  
• financial instability risk  
• fiscal sustainability

---

# Governance and Institutional Trust

### World Values Survey (WVS)
https://www.worldvaluessurvey.org

One of the most widely used datasets in social science research.

Measures:

• trust in government  
• trust in institutions  
• social cohesion  
• political attitudes  
• cultural norms

Critical dataset for testing:

• institutional legitimacy  
• societal trust networks  
• Emotional Physics hypotheses related to perception and belief systems.

---

### Edelman Trust Barometer
https://www.edelman.com/trust

Annual global survey measuring:

• trust in government  
• trust in business  
• trust in media  
• trust in NGOs

Provides insight into **global trust dynamics** and public perception of institutions.

---

### Transparency International
https://www.transparency.org

Provides the **Corruption Perceptions Index (CPI)**.

Measures perceived corruption levels across countries and is widely used in governance research.

Used in SEI testing for:

• institutional integrity  
• corruption and stability relationships

---

# Democracy and Political Stability

### Freedom House
https://freedomhouse.org

Measures:

• political rights  
• civil liberties  
• democratic governance indicators

Useful for examining the relationship between **freedom indicators and societal stability**.

---

### Varieties of Democracy (V-Dem)
https://www.v-dem.net

One of the most comprehensive global governance datasets.

Measures:

• democratic institutions  
• civil liberties  
• political participation  
• electoral integrity  
• governance structures

Frequently used in academic research examining institutional evolution.

---

# Conflict and Geopolitical Stability

### Uppsala Conflict Data Program (UCDP)
https://ucdp.uu.se

Provides detailed data on:

• armed conflicts  
• battle deaths  
• conflict actors  
• peace agreements

Critical for studying **societal instability and conflict risk**.

---

### Correlates of War Project
https://correlatesofwar.org

Provides historical data on:

• interstate wars  
• alliances  
• military capabilities  
• geopolitical conflicts

Used to analyze **historical patterns of systemic instability**.

---

# Information Environment and Media Integrity

### Reuters Digital News Report
https://www.digitalnewsreport.org

Global dataset measuring:

• media consumption patterns  
• trust in news  
• information ecosystems

Relevant to SEI variables concerning **information integrity and societal perception**.

---

### Global Disinformation Index
https://www.disinformationindex.org

Measures:

• misinformation prevalence  
• media reliability risks  
• disinformation ecosystems

Useful for studying the role of **information environments in societal stability**.

# Demographic and Population Data

### United Nations Population Division

• population growth

Used in demographic modeling within SEI frameworks.

# Inequality and Social Distribution

### World Inequality Database
https://wid.world

Provides detailed data on:

• wealth inequality  
• income distribution  
• long-term inequality trends

Useful for examining **structural inequality and systemic stability relationships**.

# Dataset Usage Principles

When datasets are used within experiments, researchers should document:

• dataset source  
• dataset version or year  
• variables used  
• preprocessing steps  
• analytical methods applied

Proper documentation ensures:

• transparency  
• reproducibility  
• scientific integrity.

# Contribution

Researchers are encouraged to propose additional datasets that may improve empirical testing of ACL frameworks.

Suggested datasets should meet the following criteria:

• publicly accessible  
• well documented  
• widely used in academic research  
• methodologically reliable
