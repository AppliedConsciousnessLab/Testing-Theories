# Research Principles

The Testing-Theories repository exists to empirically evaluate theoretical frameworks related to societal stability and systemic dynamics.

Research conducted in this repository follows several guiding principles.

## Evidence First

Hypotheses should be evaluated using real-world data wherever possible.

## Transparency

All experiments should clearly document methods, datasets, and results.

## Reproducibility

Analysis should be reproducible using documented datasets and scripts.

## Open Inquiry

Unexpected or contradictory findings are valuable and should be recorded honestly.

## Interdisciplinary Approach

This repository integrates perspectives from:

- systems theory
- sociology
- economics
- psychology
- complexity science

The goal is not to confirm theories, but to test them.
